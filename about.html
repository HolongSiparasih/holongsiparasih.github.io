About Us

At HOLONG SIPARASIH, we provide the highest quality Indonesian Robusta Coffee, processed with modern machinery to ensure excellence. We have a global reach, exporting our products to customers worldwide with a commitment to timely shipment and satisfaction. Our state-of-the-art production facility in South Sumatra (Lampung) has a capacity of 15 MT/hour, and our extensive inventory capabilities allow us to store up to 10,000 MT, supporting an annual production of 30,000 tons. Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has enabled us to capture a significant market share. We also prioritize strategic partnerships, aiming to be a long-term partner in the coffee industry by focusing on sustainability and efficient supply chain management.

Our Story

Established in 2016, HOLONG SIPARASIH is dedicated to enriching lives through the supply of high-quality agro-commodities. With a robust processing and trading framework, we are committed to sustainability and excellence in every aspect of our business.
